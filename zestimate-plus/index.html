The Problem

The Zestimate breaks trust at the most important moment

A homeowner's first move when considering selling is checking the Zestimate. When it feels wrong — and it often does — Zillow loses the relationship before it begins. The Zestimate relies on baseline comparables: square footage, bedroom count, bathroom count, and yard size. Real estate appraisers start there too — but then adjust property-by-property for renovations, finishes, location, and condition. The Zestimate stops where the real work begins. That gap is the opportunity.

🏠 Real Seller. Real Story.

"Before I even had a showing, realtors and buyers told me my price was too high. Their reference point was the Zestimate."

I listed my home with confidence — I knew what it was worth from my real estate experience and licensure. But the Zestimate was showing $300K–$325K. The pushback I received from potential buyers and realtors created mistrust and made me doubt my own valuation of my own home.

My home sold in 2 days for $362,000 — up to $62,000 above the Zestimate. The Zestimate only uses baseline comparables — square footage, beds, baths, yard size. It doesn't do what appraisers do: adjust for renovations, finishes, location premiums, and condition. Zestimate+ models that process.

Long-Term Vision

From transaction to lifelong homeowner relationship

The Seller team's mission is to foster long-term relationships with homeowners — helping them understand their home's value, unlock new possibilities, and prepare for important moments. Zestimate+ is the foundation of that relationship, built on three pillars.

01
Build Trust Through Transparency
Show homeowners exactly how their value is calculated. When they can see, challenge, and contribute to the estimate, they trust it. Trust is the currency that converts homeowners into sellers — and sellers into Zillow advocates.
02
Help Them Understand Their Home's Value
A live, personalized, seller-verified Zestimate+ becomes their authoritative source of truth. Zillow becomes the dashboard for their most valuable asset — a living valuation they understand and own, not a number they distrust.
03
Unlock New Possibilities
The Value Roadmap shows homeowners what their home could be worth. Every upgrade is a possibility unlocked. Zillow becomes a partner in building equity, not just measuring it — present across the entire ownership cycle.

Technical Approach — Value Roadmap NLP Pipeline

1
Keyword extraction from listing descriptions — NLP model identifies renovation and feature language in MLS listing descriptions for recently sold homes: "renovated kitchen," "smart home," "fresh paint," "new roof," "quartz countertops," "designer finishes," etc.
2
Price delta modeling per keyword cluster — For a given zip code and price band, calculate the average sale price premium for homes with vs. without each feature keyword cluster. Weight by recency and comp similarity score.
3
Personalized ranking for this seller's comp set — Surface the highest-ROI upgrades specific to this homeowner's actual comparable homes — not national averages. Pull the exact listing description quotes that inform each recommendation for full transparency.
4
Continuous refresh as new comps sell — As new comparable homes close in the area, the Value Roadmap updates automatically. The homeowner receives a nudge: "3 homes near you just sold. Your roadmap has been updated." This drives ongoing re-engagement across the full ownership cycle.
Key advantage: Zillow already has all the data required — MLS listing descriptions, sale prices, comp relationships, and zip-level market data. This feature requires no new data acquisition. It's smarter surfacing of existing assets.
